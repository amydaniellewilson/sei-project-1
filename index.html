PULP FICTON
MASTERMIND

Rules:

- The computer chooses a sequence of four colours. - The objective of the game is to guess the exact positions of the colours in the computer’s sequence.
- After you select four colours for your guess, the computer will give you a hint on the right hand side of how close you are.
- If you have any of the colours correct and they are in the right place, you will get a number of red pegs to alert how many you have right.
- If you have any of the colours correct and they are not in the right place, you will get a number of white pegs to alert how many you have right.
- If the rest of the pegs will stay black for however many you have not guessed correctly.
- You lose the game if you use all 10 attempts without guessing the computer code sequence.
- You win the game when you manage to guess all the colours in the code sequence and when they are all in the right position.

How to play this game:

- Start your first game by pressing the ‘Play’ button. - To start filing a line, just click on the colour of your choice.
- When you have filed colours, the computer will tell you what you’ve got right and wrong so you can continue guessing on each line.
- To reset the game, press the reset button.
